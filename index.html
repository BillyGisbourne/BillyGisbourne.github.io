Who am I?

I am Billy Gisbourne, a Second Year Graphic Design Student and am currently studying at Harlow College.

My biggest aspirations are to become a well established Web Designer, working either with a company, or being self employed. To achieve my goal I will gain my degree in Web Design and go from there.

I am also positively interested in Game Design, but I have decided against that career path as I feel I am not strong enough in that field to be superiorly successful .

My skills

  • Photoshop

    Skill:

    Throughout my life of design, this program has been my go-to, due to it's extremely diverse capabilities. I have been using Photoshop since the age of 10, using it to create and manipulate artwork aswell as basic webdesign.

  • Dreamweaver

    Skill:

    I have only recently started to use Dreamweaver; I began by using Notepad to write HTML and CSS. I thoroughly enjoy coding websites, primarily because of the amount of creativity needed, even though I am still learning HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Because I eventually want a career in Web Design I will need to become more fluent with these languages.

  • Illustrator

    Skill:

    I don't always use Illustrator, but when I do, I use it majestically.
